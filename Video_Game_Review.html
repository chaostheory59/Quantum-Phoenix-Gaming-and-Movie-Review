Middle Earth: Shadow Of War

Middle Earth Shadow of War is an Action Role-PLaying Game developed by Monolith Studios. The game is a sequel to the Middle Earth: Shadow of Mordor game where the player plays as a ranger of Gondor who was killed but was brought back to life by tying his body to the soul of Calebrimbor.  Calebrimbor is an Elven Forge Lord who forged the rings of Sauron. Shadow of War takes the ranger to Mount Doom where he forges a new ring to fight against the dark armies of Sauron and to exact revenge. From planning a seige battle to fighting the entire Nazgul, the game offers a lot of entertainment. The game mechanics is pretty fluid and smooth. The revenge and nemsis system has gotten so much better along as the personality of each Uruk that you come across. The down side to the game is the fact that the graphics does not look like it has improved that much nor do i feel that the game has a lot of content which forces the players to buy the DLC or Season pass to fully enjoy the game.

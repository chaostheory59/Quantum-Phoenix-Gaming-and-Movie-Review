Quantum Pheonixs Gaming Review

My Quantum Phoenix Gaming & Movie Review website will provide an interactive forum for entertaining and informative reviews and exchanges relating to video games, movies and the inside culture of its followers. It aims to be a uniquely designed and exciting website that appeals to an audience that I understand well. While there are many game and movie review websites, Quantum will target a 18-30 year old segment and will use humor and inside jokes that appeal to this close-knit audience.

The website will feature comment forums throughout so that the target audience feels “part of show.” In this description I will be discussing the features, playability, the flow, story, mechanics, graphics and more. At the same time, I plan to show clips that I will take from games that I review.
